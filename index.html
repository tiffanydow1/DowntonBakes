All About Downton Bakes

Hi I’m Sara, twenty-seven, messy baker, can never reach the top shelf. My Mom let me bake my first batch of cookies when I was two and possibly regretted it immediately when she saw the flour on the ceiling. I’m currently still baking in her kitchen, and there is still flour on the ceiling. Sorry Mom. What I’m trying to say is I’ve been baking as long as I can remember, and I’m alway up to create a pretty cake. Or cookie. Or cupcake. (You get the idea) So, welcome! Have a look around! Hope to be baking for you soon.
